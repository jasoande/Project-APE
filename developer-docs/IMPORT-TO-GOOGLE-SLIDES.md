## 🎨 Quick Formatting Guide

### Slide 1 (Title)
- King Kong logo: CENTER, HUGE (60% slide height)
- Title: Below logo, Montserrat Bold 60pt, White
- Subtitle: Below title, 32pt, Gold `#d4af37`

### Slides 2-10
- Title: Montserrat Bold 48pt, White
- Body: Open Sans 18pt, White
- Numbers/Metrics: Roboto Mono 32pt, Gold `#d4af37`
- Emojis: Keep them! (📊 💰 ✅ etc.)

### Colors to Use
```
Navy Blue:  #1a1f3a (background top)
Black:      #0a0e1a (background bottom)  
Gold:       #d4af37 (accents, numbers)
White:      #ffffff (text)
Cyan:       #00d9ff (highlights)
```
