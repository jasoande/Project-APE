Act as a $persona and strategic advisor for $name.

## Part 1: Strategic Solution Ideas

Brainstorm 10 diverse, concrete ideas for how Red Hat technology and services can benefit $name in achieving their objectives and overcoming their challenges.

**For each idea, provide:**

1. **Idea Title:** Short, descriptive name
2. **Business Objective Alignment:** Which $name objective(s) does this support?
3. **Customer Challenge:** What specific problem does this solve?
4. **Red Hat Solution:** Which RH technology/service is deployed?
   - RHEL / OpenShift / Ansible / Red Hat AI / Advanced Cluster Security
5. **Success Metrics:** How does this influence $name's KPIs?
6. **Partner Recommendation:** Which RH IT partner could support implementation?
7. **Implementation Complexity:** Low / Medium / High

**Make ideas diverse across:**
- Different business units
- Various technology domains (infrastructure, automation, AI, security, containers)
- Range of complexity and timeline
- Mix of tactical and strategic initiatives

## Part 2: "How Might We..." Innovation Workshop

Generate 15 "How might we..." (HMW) statements that break $name's key initiatives into actionable opportunities.

**For each HMW statement:**
- **Statement:** "How might we help $name [specific opportunity]?"
- **Concrete Action:** One specific project or initiative
- **Primary Red Hat Technology:** Which product applies
- **Expected Outcome:** Business impact

**Examples:**
- "How might we help $name accelerate application modernization?"
  - Action: Migrate legacy apps to containers on OpenShift
  - Technology: OpenShift Container Platform
  - Outcome: 40% faster deployment cycles

Format: Clear numbering, concise statements. Total: 2-3 pages.
